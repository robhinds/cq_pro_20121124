About Me



"Become a freelance copywriter," they said,


"and spend your afternoons on the golf course."



Don't believe it.


Freelance copywriting is a difficult, demanding, hugely rewarding job. Some of us even get up before 8 a.m.

I have a first-class degree in English Literature, a diploma in Copywriting, and a working background in PR (which trained me in getting to the point) and Marketing (where I learned how to Sex Things Up). Unfortunately, I've too much drive and independence to stay at one company for long.

So I built my own.

And I really love it.

My copywriting work is amazingly varied, covering everything from diamonds to lost dogs. Words can be brilliant... in the right hands. Take a look at some of my interesting clients, or contact me to ask for a price or samples.

My Fees


Copywriters can charge by the hour or project, working on a monthly retainer or fixed project. Ask whether revisions are included in the initial quote. Copywriting fees start at £25.00 per hour and will depend on your copywriter's experience, outgoings and confidence, and the market.


WHAT'S MINE?

It's not a secret. Just contact me with your requirements and I'll supply a fixed price for your project.

It's not a nuisance, it's not difficult, and it's probably not as expensive as you'd think. (See my blog article on choosing a copywriter and copywriting fees.) Can't afford it? I might be able to hook you up with some free copywriting.
